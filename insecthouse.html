INSECT HOUSE

The insect house is a fascinating section in the zoo that showcases a diverse collection of insects from around the world. Visitors can observe a variety of colorful and exotic insects, including butterflies, spiders, beetles, and ants, up close and learn about their behaviors, habitats, and unique characteristics.

The insect house provides a fun and educational experience for all ages and is a great way to discover the incredible world of insects.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?
